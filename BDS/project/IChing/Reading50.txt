50  Ting  鼎

The upper trigram is Li, the lightness, and the lower trigram is Sun, the gentleness, a metaphor for the rise of all things. Ding (鼎) is the most auspicious object for worshipping heaven. Remove the old way and replace it with the new one. Even if there are still obstacles in your way, you can enjoy the results of your efforts once you have been patient and persevered.
